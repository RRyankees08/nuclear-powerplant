Try Managing a Nuclear Power Plant!

Explore the challenges of balancing safety, efficiency, and energy production in this interactive simulation. Use the arrow keys to change the position of the control rods, regulating the fission process. Manage the water level, and refill cooling supply as necessary to avoid going into meltdown.
